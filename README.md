# Prime Directive

A comprehensive markdown library of AI governance, risk, and compliance prompts designed to help organizations and individuals establish baseline directives for responsible AI deployment.

## Overview

Prime Directive is a curated collection of structured prompts that guide AI agents toward the greater good. These prompts help companies and teams:

- **Establish AI Governance** — Define policies and oversight structures
- **Manage Risk** — Identify and mitigate potential harms and misuse
- **Ensure Compliance** — Navigate regulatory frameworks and standards
- **Promote Safety** — Embed responsible AI practices into workflows
